User Privacy Choices

This app does not collect or share personal data.

You can control the following privacy-related features:

  • You can disable Siri interactions with this app at any time in iOS Settings → Siri & Search.
  • You can turn off Bluetooth access for this app in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Bluetooth.

This page explains how you can manage privacy-related settings on your device. The app itself does not maintain separate user profiles or tracking settings.
