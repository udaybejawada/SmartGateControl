SmartRelayControl – Support

Thank you for using SmartRelayControl, a small utility app that lets you control a compatible Bluetooth relay for your vehicle gate or tailgate.

1. Basic Requirements

  • iPhone or iPad running a supported version of iOS / iPadOS.
  • A compatible Bluetooth relay module installed and powered.
  • Vehicle or gate wiring done safely by a qualified person.

2. Getting Started

  1. Install SmartRelayControl from the App Store.
  2. Turn on Bluetooth on your iOS device.
  3. Power your relay module and place it within range.
  4. Open the app and select the relay from the device list.
  5. Use the available buttons to trigger the desired gate functions.

3. Safety Notice

Always make sure the area around the gate or tailgate is clear before activating it. You are responsible for complying with local safety rules and vehicle manufacturer recommendations.

4. Troubleshooting

  • The relay is not listed: check power to the relay and make sure Bluetooth is enabled.
  • The gate does not move: verify wiring, fuses, and that the relay contacts are correctly connected.
  • Connection drops: reduce distance, remove obstacles, and make sure only one phone is connected.
